Rumble

HOW TO PLAY


Guess the RUMBLE in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

The word bank includes five-letter characters, locations, objects, and concepts from Attack on Titan. If you need inspiration, check out the Attack on Titan Wiki

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


Examples


T I T A N

The letter T is in the word and in the correct spot.

E R W I N

The letter R is in the word, but in the wrong spot.

T R O S T

The letter S is not in the word in any spot.


A new RUMBLE will be available each day!
